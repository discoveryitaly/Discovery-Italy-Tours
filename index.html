How it works

Every experience
is a building block.

Italy isn't one destination — it's a hundred. And that's exactly why it's hard to sell. Most agents stick to Rome, Florence, Venice. Not because there's nothing else. Because no one has given them a map.

We have.

Discovery Italy Tours organizes every Italian experience into three categories: Food & Wine, Art & Culture, and Outdoor. Within each category, the experiences are modular — like building blocks. You pick the pieces that match your client's interests. We assemble the trip.

It's not a package. It's not a template. It's a tour that belongs to your client.

Why travel advisors choose us

We're in two countries.
So nothing gets lost.

01

Your team in Italy — 24/7.

Throughout the tour, your clients always have someone in Italy. Not a call center. A concierge team that knows the tour, the suppliers, and the local contingencies. It protects your client. It protects your reputation.

02

Transparent, agent-first pricing.

We quote once the itinerary is complete — a single all-inclusive price for the full tour. Agent commission is agreed during the planning phase and built directly into the quote. Clean, clear, no surprises.

03

A DMC with a full-Italy catalog.

We work directly with individual local operators across every destination we cover — personally selected, personally visited. No intermediaries, no wholesalers. The supplier your client meets on the ground is the same one we know by name.
