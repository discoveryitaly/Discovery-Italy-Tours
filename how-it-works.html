Contact us
Do you sell directly to travelers?

No. We work exclusively through US travel advisors. If a traveler contacts us directly, we refer them to a registered partner in our network. Your relationship with your client is protected.

What commission structure do you offer?

We quote once the itinerary is complete — a single all-inclusive price for the full tour. Agent commission is agreed during the planning phase: we ask which percentage you want to include and build it directly into the final quote. Your margin is protected by design.

How quickly can you turn around a custom quote?

Within 24 hours for most requests. For complex multi-destination itineraries or large groups, allow up to 48 hours. We'll always confirm receipt of your request within the hour.

Do you cover all of Italy or just certain regions?

Our core catalog covers Sicily, Amalfi Coast, Tuscany, Rome, Lake Como, Food Valley (Emilia-Romagna), and Milan. We can arrange tours to other Italian destinations on request — just ask.

What happens if something goes wrong during the tour?

Our Italy team is reachable during every tour. We have contingency plans for the most common issues — from weather disruptions to supplier no-shows. Your client will always have someone to call. And so will you.

Can I book a fam trip to experience your tours firsthand?

Yes — and we encourage it. We run fam trips regularly throughout the year for registered partners. They're the best way to sell Italy with real conviction. Ask us about the next one.
