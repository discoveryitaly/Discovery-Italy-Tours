The story

It starts in Sicily.

Vincenzo Ciceraro spent years building Chat and Tour Srl — a Palermo-based incoming tour operator he launched in 2001 that became one of the most trusted DMC operations in southern Italy. No billboards. No flashy marketing. Just 20+ years of making things work on the ground, building a network of operators across the entire Italian peninsula.

Sebastiano Peluso was on a different path — deep inside the US market, understanding how American advisors work, what they need, and how they sell. He brought the American perspective. Vincenzo brought Italy. In 2017 they started testing a simple thesis together.

In 2023, Discovery Italy Tours Inc. was incorporated in New York. Two offices. A shared catalog covering all of Italy. A team that has now grown to include Simona Ciceraro, who joined that same year and manages the day-to-day relationship with US travel agents from our Smithtown, NY office.

Over $1.5 million in private tours sold. Seven destinations. A model that works — because it was tested before it was formalized.

Five things that make us different

Two continents, one team

New York and Palermo. We understand the American way of selling travel and know Italy the way only those born and working there can. No time zone panic. No cultural guesswork.

Agent-first pricing

We quote once the itinerary is complete — one all-inclusive price for the full tour. Commission is agreed during planning and built into the quote. Your margin is never at risk.

Concierge team — 24/7

Throughout every tour, your clients have a real person in Italy. A team that knows the tour, the suppliers, and the contingencies. Your client never feels alone. Neither do you.

Proven since 2001

Chat and Tour Srl has operated in Sicily since 2001. Discovery Italy LLC has worked with the US market since 2017. $1.5M+ in private tours sold before we even incorporated. The model works — we have the receipts.

A DMC — not a wholesaler

We work directly with individual local operators across every destination we cover — personally selected, personally visited, no intermediaries. The supplier your client will meet on the ground is the same one we've built a direct relationship with. No generic catalogs. No bus tours. Just Italy at its best.
