Our story

Born in Sicily.
Built for America.

Discovery Italy Tours is the story of two friends who refused to believe that Italy had to be sold badly.

🇮🇹

"We built the bridge between the America that wants Italy and the Italy worth discovering."

— Sebastiano Peluso, Co-Founder

The story

It starts in Sicily.

Vincenzo Ciceraro spent years building Chat and Tour Srl — a Palermo-based incoming tour operator he launched in 2001 that became, quietly, one of the most trusted DMC operations in southern Italy. No billboards. No flashy marketing. Just 20+ years of making things work on the ground, building a network of suppliers across all of Italy.

Sebastiano Peluso was on a different path — deep inside the US startup ecosystem, working with Italian entrepreneurs trying to crack the American market. He understood how business gets done on this side of the Atlantic. Not just English, but the language of American commerce, American expectations, American clients.

In 2017 they started working together with a simple thesis: the US travel market wanted Italy — authentic, curated, boutique Italy — but no one was connecting American advisors to the right operators in a seamless, professional way.

So they built the bridge.

Discovery Italy Tours was incorporated in New York in 2023. Today we operate from two offices: New York and Palermo. We sell exclusively through US travel advisors. We work only with boutique, personally-vetted Italian operators.

The team

Meet the founders

SP
Sebastiano Peluso
PRESIDENT & CO-FOUNDER — NEW YORK

Sebastiano has spent his career at the intersection of Italian business and the American market. After years working with Italian startups and entrepreneurs expanding into the US, he turned his attention to travel — a sector he believed was overdue for a better model. He brings the US-market instinct: he knows what American advisors need, how they sell, and what their clients expect.

When he's not building new destination experiences or leading agent webinars, you'll find him plotting which Sicilian street food he's going to eat next.

VC
Vincenzo Ciceraro
CO-FOUNDER & ITALY OPERATIONS — PALERMO

Vincenzo has been on the ground in Italy since 2001, when he founded Chat and Tour Srl in Palermo. Over two decades he built one of Sicily's most respected incoming operations — a network of suppliers, guides, properties, and experiences that spans the entire island and extends across the Italian peninsula.

He is the reason our tours actually work. He knows which guide will make your client's trip unforgettable. He knows the estate that doesn't appear in any catalogue. He knows Italy — not from a desk, but from 20+ years of living and working in it.

Behind Sebastiano and Vincenzo is a team of travel designers, local guides, and destination experts operating across Italy. Every experience in our catalog has been personally tested. Every supplier has been visited in person. We don't sell what we haven't seen.

Our philosophy

We don't do generic.

"Italy is not a backdrop. It's not a checklist of landmarks to photograph and leave behind. Italy is a living thing — a place of flavors, arguments, family recipes, inexplicable beauty, and the kind of joy that only comes from doing something the old-fashioned way."

Our job is to put your clients inside that world. Not in front of it.

That means cooking with the nonna who won't share her recipe with anyone else. Sailing along the Sicilian coast at sunset with an aperitivo in hand. Driving a vintage Fiat 500 through the Chianti hills with no agenda. Eating street food in Palermo's Ballarò market because the best things in Italy have never been in a Michelin guide.

We build these experiences for your clients. And we make you look brilliant in the process.
