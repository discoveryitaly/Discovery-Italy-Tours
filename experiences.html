What kind of Italy
are you looking for?

Every experience we offer falls into one of three categories. Start here, then mix and match across destinations.

🍷
Category 01

Food & Wine

Italy's greatest pleasure is its table. Cooking classes with local families, truffle hunts in the hills, wine tastings on private estates, street food tours at dawn, market visits before the tourists arrive, and dinners that last three hours because no one wants them to end.

Cooking classes Wine tastings & estates Truffle hunts Street food tours Dinner with locals Market visits
Book a Food & Wine experience
🏛
Category 02

Art & Culture

Italy is the world's largest open-air museum — but the best of it isn't behind velvet ropes. Private guided walks through ancient neighborhoods, archaeological sites before the crowds, artisan workshops where old crafts still live, palaces that haven't been in any guidebook.

Guided city walks Archaeological sites Museum tours Artisan workshops Historic palaces Private access
Book an Art & Culture experience
🚵
Category 03

Outdoor

Italy's landscapes are as extraordinary as its cities. Hiking the Gods' Path above the Amalfi Coast, kayaking along Cinque Terre's cliffs, cycling through Chianti vineyards, sailing at sunset off Sicily — experiences that can't be replicated in any hotel lobby or museum queue.

Hiking & trekking Boat tours Kayaking Cycling Sailing Nature walks
